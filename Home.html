Censorship

Censorship is defined as the act of suppressing speech or writing that is believed to be harmful to an individual or group. Censorship is a controversial issue today and is most commonly seen in authoritative states such as North Korea, China, and Russia. However, censorship is also seen in America on popular social media platforms such as YouTube, Twitter, and Facebook.

Throughout history, censorship in the media has been used, as a way to manipulate the public opinions and beliefs. This is why it is such an important topic to discuses and learn about. The purpose of this site it to inform and educate the viewers to help limit the dangers and implications that censorship might pose on a free society.
