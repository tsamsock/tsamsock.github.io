FAQs

Q: Why is there so much censorship and bias in social media?

A: The legal loopholes today allow for social media platforms to censor any user they wish for any reason they give. Most platforms cite their user agreement policies as backing for bans.

Q: Why are we in such a heated war of words today?

A: Social media platforms use their monopoly on communication and connection to dictate what their users are allowed to say. This creates a divide in groups and a strain on peoples range of free speech.

Q: How can we handle censorship in social media?

A: Our governmnet could pass more detailed laws that prohibit censoring. Additionally, other social media platform could be crated to compete with the monopolies and limit their control.
