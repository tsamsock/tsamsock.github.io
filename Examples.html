Examples Of Censorship Today

  • YouTube removing videos opposing vaccine or other Covid-19 guidelines. Even debates between prestigious individuals have been removed.

  • Facebook bans Australian media outlets and censors users from sharing or viewing news articles from sources it decides.

  • Twitter and Facebook ban New York Post for posting articles that contradict Twitter's opinions and political leaning.

  • YouTube helps China censor any comments in oppostition of the Chinese Communist Party.
